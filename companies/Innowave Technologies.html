InnoWave is a technology and IT services company, specializing in BSS systems (CRM and Billing), OSS systems (Network Provisioning and Service Fulfilment) and Multimedia services and products (Interactive TV applications, Mobile applications, Automated Device Testing). InnoWave has a strong international outlook and is committed to continuous innovation so that our customers can benefit from "Solutions That Simply Work". Based in Lisbon, Portugal and with offices in Brussels and London, InnoWave is employing a Global Service Delivery model with customers and projects in several European countries, the Americas and Asia.
